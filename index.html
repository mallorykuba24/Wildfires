These Girls are on Firrreeee.

California is one of the places having the most deadliest and destructive wildfire seasons. The dataset contains the list of Wildfires that has occurred in California between 2013 and 2020. The dataset contains the location where wildfires have occurred including the County name, latitude and longitude values and also details on when the wildfire has started. 40 columns. This data helps to generate insights on what locations in California are under fire threat, what time do Wildfires usually occur and how frequent and devastating they are!!.

MAP

Each circle is plotted by latitude and longitude coordinates from the data set. Each circle's radius is determined by the amount of acres burned in that area. The color intensity is plotted to show the length of time the fire burned in hours.
